About me

My name is Zach Pruitt and I was born and raised in the small town of Kernersville, North Carolina.

Growing up I enjoyed playing baseball, golf, football, basketball and hockey. I was also an avid dirtbike rider, competing in Hare-Scramble events in the mountains on the weekends. My hobbies include playing guitar, reading, skim-boarding, kayaking, fishing, hiking, running, carpentry, and all things sports.

In my spare time I enjoy hanging out with friends, family, my girlfriend Kristina and our three dogs; Zoso, Karma and Suki. Kristina and I love to take the pups walking and hiking, and also enjoy spontaneous trips to the beach and mountains.

$("#calculate").click(function () {
            //step1: get user data
            var num1 = Number($("#input1").val());
            var num2 = Number($("#input2").val());
            var num3 = Number($("#input3").val());
            var num4 = Number($("#input4").val());
            var num5 = Number($("#input5").val());

            //step2: make calculation with data
            var min = Math.min(num1, num2, num3, num4, num5);
            var max = Math.max(num1, num2, num3, num4, num5);
            var avg = ((num1 + num2 + num3 + num4 + num5) / 5);
            var sum = num1 + num2 + num3 + num4 + num5;
            var product = (num1 * num2 * num3 * num4 * num5);


            //step3:
            var minOutput = "The smallest of your numbers is " + min + ".";
            $("#resultMin").text(minOutput);

            var maxOutput = "The largest of your numbers is " + max + ".";
            $("#resultMax").text(maxOutput);

            var avgOutput = "The average of your numbers is " + avg + ".";
            $("#resultAvg").text(avgOutput);

            var sumOutput = "The sum of your numbers is " + sum + ".";
            $("#resultSum").text(sumOutput);

            var productOutput = "The product of your numbers is " + product + ".";
            $("#resultProduct").text(productOutput);
        })
        $("#clear").click(function () {

            $("#input1,#input2,#input3,#input4,#input5").val("");

            $("#resultMin").text("");
            $("#resultMax").text("");
            $("#resultAvg").text("");
            $("#resultSum").text("");
            $("#resultProduct").text("");
        });

- For each number that is a multiple of your second entry, the word "Buzz" will populate.

- For each number that is a multiple of both entries, the word "FizzBuzz" will populate.

Palindrome Exercise

This exercise is used to determine if the Users' input is a Palindrome.

A Palindrome is a word, phrase, or sequence that reads the same backwards as it does forward.

For example; dad, pop, and mom, are all Palindromes.

Give it a try!

Factorial Exercise

This exercise is used to determine the factorial of a given number.

A Factorial is the product of an integer and all the integers below it.

For example, the factorial of 4 is 24...(4*3*2*1) = 24

Give it a try!
